Research Summary

I have always been intrigued by how the idea of replicating human intelligence can solve complex problems more efficiently. Specifically, in engineering research, some difficult problems often require computationally heavy solutions.

My research focuses on the use of machine learning approaches for science and engineering applications, such as surrogate modeling1 of complex simulated systems to bring the computational time down to a practical timeframe.

Beyond this, I also explore computer vision ranging from segmention2, GANs, vision transformers to stable diffusion3.
